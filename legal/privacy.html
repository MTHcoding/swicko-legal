Who We Are

Swicko & Souro is a gamified wellness companion app developed as an independent app. This privacy policy explains how the app handles information.

Effective date: February 23, 2026
Contact: privacy@swickoandsouro.app

What This App Is

Swicko & Souro is a game. You help a fictional cherry character named Swicko earn shield points by logging meals. This app is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnoses, or treatment.

Data We Collect

We collect no personal data. There are no accounts, no cloud sync, no analytics, no advertising identifiers, and no servers that receive your information.

All data you enter (meals, shield scores, streaks, preferences) is stored exclusively in your device's local storage using AsyncStorage and is never transmitted off your device.

Camera Permission

The app requests camera access solely to scan barcodes on food product packaging. Photos are never captured, stored, or transmitted. The camera session is only active while you are actively scanning a barcode.
